Who we are · 01

In Brahmanism, Līlā is the cosmic play. Creation, not as effort but as expression.

Our symbol is a putto riding two snakes. One serpent is fear, the other curiosity. Creation happens in between.

Too much fear kills ideas. Too much curiosity without control creates chaos. We ride both seriously, but never solemnly.
